Our Star Sell

InstaGame – social network game

Do you want to rule the social media world? Do you want to have a massive following? Do you love social media? Well, you’ll love InstaGame – social network game. It gives you the ability to post content in the form of stories and posts and get free rewards to build a fantastic profile with millions of followers. It mixes the best features of role-playing games and social media games into one fun in the game.

NUCLEAR REACTION

Are you ready for a real 'brain teaser' and 'mind puzzle' challenge? Try Nuclear Reaction, a fun strategy puzzle game for 2 to 8 players. This free multiplayer puzzle game can be enjoyed with friends both online and also offline locally. With global profiles, you can battle in a real strategy mind puzzle game against players from all around the world. ​Do you have what it takes to become the best in Nuclear Reaction?

TURNY ROAD

Are you ready for new and fresh racing excitement? Well, welcome to a crazy fun & ultra-fast zig zag racer pursuit. Turny Road will take you on the right of your life. Stunning setting, gorgeous graphics and driving engine that you’ve probably never tried before. Ideal for the racing enthusiasts in all of us. Warning: this zig zag racing gameplay is amazing addictive! You’ll may want more and more. ​
